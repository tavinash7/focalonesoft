IT Consulting/Staffing

Having a workforce that is capable of managing successful business operations is very critical for all industries. Focalonesoft clearly understands the need of its industry partners to find the most suitable person at the right time and cost for any given task.

Focalonesoft always presents seasoned professionals with industry-relevant project implementation expertise in cutting-edge technologies. All our professionals are chosen after rigorous tests, interviews, and reference/background checks. They are vendor-certified in their respective skill and have impeccable technical, written, and communication skills. Most importantly, each of our consultants follows the ‘Customer First’ culture, which is the hallmark of our company.

Our stringent and effective recruitment methodologies have enabled us to augment staff in the areas of:

  • Applications Development
  • Systems Management
  • Project Management
  • Networking Support and Management
  • Business Operations support
  • Business Process Analysis
  • Data Management
  • Data Warehousing/Business Intelligence
  • Quality Assurance

Depending on the needs of clients, we provide the following ::

  • Contract Consulting/Staffing: We provide consultants after establishing a contract with the client for all staffing needs. This could happen for one consultant or an entire team for a specific project. Task orders are issued for individual assignments.
  • Temporary Staffing: We provide staff to fill up temporary skill gaps arising from maternity and sick leave, peak and/or seasonal workloads in business administration, financial, and accounting, and/or a new technology project requiring a specific IT skill.
  • Direct Hire: We help our clients in finding the right candidates for their direct hire needs. We find, source, and pre-interview the candidates based on the specifications given and then present the results to our clients. This helps our clients reduce their recruitment cycle and saves them time in identifying the most suitable candidate.
  • Contract to Hire: We also give flexibility to our clients in testing a potential employee first before doing a permanent hire. This service enables the client to judge and analyze the candidate in real-time environment before making their decision.
